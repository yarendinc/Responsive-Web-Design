Frequently Asked Questions

What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It includes learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

Which programming language is best for AI development?

Python is the most popular language for AI development due to its simplicity, large number of libraries like TensorFlow, PyTorch, and scikit-learn, and strong community support.

How can I start learning machine learning?

You can begin by learning Python, understanding basic statistics and linear algebra, and exploring online platforms like Coursera, edX, and Kaggle for ML courses and datasets.
